Arabica Coffee

Arabica coffee beans tend to taste sweet, fruity, floral, chocolaty, and have low acidity.

Robusta Coffee

Robusta coffee tastes earthy and is often said to have a bitter, rubbery/grain-like flavor, with a peanutty aftertaste.

Liberica Coffee

Sharp stinging, with a more viscous bitter taste. Usually, Liberica coffee is mixed with milk to cover the sharp aroma and bitter taste of the coffee.

Excelsa Coffee

It has a sweet, fruity, tart quality but is strong and dark. Producers are often used to add depth of flavor and a fuller body to coffee blends.
